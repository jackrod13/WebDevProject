Our Mission

Inspire, Equipt, Educate

  • Inspire. We inspire people, through media and events, to be serious about being prepared to defend themselves and others, should they need to.
  • Equipt. We equipt people by selling some of our favorite high end gear.
  • Educate. We educate people by providing free training materials to anyone on our website and social media page. In the future we will be providing in person training classes at our ranges in southern and northern utah.

Our goal is to be much like the already exhisting company T-Rexs Arms. However, they are located in Tenessee, we wanted to be the west coast version of their company. We want to be the source of knowledge for modern minute-man. We don't just want to be another defensive equiptment shop, we want to be the hub of information, equiptment, and community.
